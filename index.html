FAQs

Q: What is the market place and what does it mean to move the cards along the arrow?
A: The market place is the area where the new cards are paced. There is always a total of 8 cards in the market place. The cards in the market place move from the market deck towards the trash pile. At the end of a players turn, slide the cards within the market place in the direction of the trash pile. Then, fill up the market place from the market deck.
The game material does not include a board for the market place. However, we do offer a printable version of the market place. You can download it here.

About

This is Habitable, a fun deck-building game where players try to find planets, make them habitable, keep them habitable, and make life flourish. We are an international team of astrophysicists who love board games and also care about the Earth. If you have feedback or comments on this game, please share them with us.
